# 📊 Retail Sales Data Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing a retail company’s sales dataset to understand overall business performance.  
The main objective was to:
- Identify top-performing product categories  
- Find regions generating higher revenue and profit  
- Analyze monthly sales trends  
Based on the analysis, business recommendations were provided to improve profitability and decision-making.

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Python  
- Pandas  
- Matplotlib  
These tools were used for data cleaning, grouping, aggregation, and visualization.

## 🔎 Analysis Performed
1. Sales vs Profit by Category  
2. Sales vs Profit by Region  
3. Monthly Sales Trend Analysis  
Bar charts were used to compare sales and profit across categories and regions, and a line chart was created to observe monthly trends.

## 💡 Business Insights
- Identified categories contributing the most to revenue and profit  
- Evaluated regional performance differences  
- Observed patterns in monthly sales  
- Suggested areas where the company can focus to improve profit margins  

## 🚀 Conclusion
This project demonstrates how basic data analysis and visualization can support business decisions.  
By understanding which categories and regions perform best, the company can allocate resources more effectively and improve overall profitability.
Through this project, I strengthened my skills in data analysis, visualization, and converting raw data into meaningful business insights.
